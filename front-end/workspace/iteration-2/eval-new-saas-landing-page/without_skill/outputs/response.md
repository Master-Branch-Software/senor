## Design Direction

Given your "calm and professional" feel target, I'd suggest:

- **Color palette**: Muted blues or slate grays with a single brand accent (teal or indigo works well for B2B SaaS)
- **Typography**: A clean sans-serif like Inter or Geist for both headings and body
- **Spacing**: Generous whitespace — don't cram features, let sections breathe
- **Imagery**: Your product screenshots are key. Show real data/UI, not abstract graphics.
- **Avoid**: Loud gradients, neon colors, excessive animations

## Tech Stack Recommendation

For a 3-week deadline:

- **Framework**: Next.js (fast to build, great for SEO, easy to deploy)
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS (speeds up layout significantly)
- **Hosting**: Vercel (one-click deploys, works perfectly with Next.js)
- **Analytics**: Plausible or PostHog (privacy-friendly, easy to add)
